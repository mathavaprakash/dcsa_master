About the Department

The Department of Computer Science and Applications, established in 1996, offers Post-Graduate Programmes in Computer Applications (MCA) and Information Technology (M.Sc.IT) that lead to highly rewarding careers. Besides, the Department also offers research degrees M.Phil. and Ph.D in Computer Science to strengthen the research dimension. The Department has taken up several sponsored research projects by UGC, ICMR and DST. In addition, the Department is also a proud recipient of UGC Indo-US 21st Century Knowlwdge Initiative Award. The mission of the Department is to provide its students with the highest quality education and professional experiences, to achieve excellence in their career.
